About Psionic Research

Independent Research Archive

What We Do

Psionic Research is an independent research archive focused on remote viewing, anomalous cognition, and the government programs that studied them. From Project STARGATE's 23-year run at Stanford Research Institute to the ongoing scientific investigation of non-local perception, this site exists to make the primary source material accessible, contextualized, and useful.

The field of anomalous cognition sits at the intersection of intelligence history, consciousness science, and experimental psychology. It has generated thousands of pages of declassified documents, dozens of peer-reviewed studies, and a body of operational results that most people have never seen. We are building the archive that should have existed years ago.

Editorial Standards

Every article published on this site is sourced from declassified government documents, peer-reviewed research, or verified firsthand testimony. We do not publish speculation. We do not promote belief systems. The goal is scientific framing applied to a subject that has historically attracted too little of it.

Where evidence is strong, we say so. Where evidence is incomplete or contested, we say that too. Readers deserve to know what the record actually shows, not what any particular camp wants it to show.

The Psionic Product Family

Psionic Research is one part of a broader effort to bring rigor and modern tooling to this field.

Psionic Research (this site) is the archive. Long-form analysis of declassified programs, historical case studies, and methodological deep dives into the protocols that produced real results.

Psionic Assist is an AI-scored Controlled Remote Viewing training application. It implements blind target protocols, structured session workflows, and machine learning-based evaluation of viewer submissions. It is the practical counterpart to the research published here.

Psionic Training (coming soon) will offer structured coursework for developing remote viewing skills from the ground up, built on the same protocols and research that inform everything else we publish.

Who We Are

Psionic Research is produced by a team of researchers and developers investigating the intersection of consciousness science and artificial intelligence. Our work is grounded in the documented record, and our tools are built to help people engage with these protocols directly rather than taking anyone's word for what they can or cannot do.
