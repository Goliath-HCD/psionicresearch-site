Privacy Policy

Effective May 13, 2026

Psionic Research ("we," "us," "our") operates the website psionicresearch.com. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Information We Collect

Email addresses. If you subscribe to our newsletter or research dispatches, we collect the email address you provide. This is the only piece of personal information we ask for.

Analytics data. We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site. GA4 collects anonymized usage data including pages visited, time on site, referring sources, device type, and general geographic region. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

How We Use Your Data

Email addresses are used exclusively to send research dispatches, article notifications, and occasional updates about Psionic Research and related products. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

Analytics data is used to understand site traffic patterns, identify which content is most useful to readers, and improve the site. We do not use analytics data to track individual users across the web.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate this site:

Google Analytics 4. Provided by Google LLC. GA4 processes anonymized usage data to generate traffic reports. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by GA4. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

MailerLite. Our email newsletter is managed through MailerLite. When you subscribe, your email address is stored on MailerLite's servers. MailerLite's privacy policy governs their handling of that data. We use MailerLite solely to send you the content you signed up for.

Cookies

This site uses a limited number of cookies:

Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _ga_*) are used to distinguish unique visitors and track session information. These cookies expire after 2 years and 24 hours, respectively.

Essential cookies may be set to support basic site functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies beyond those required by Google Analytics.

You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect analytics accuracy but will not limit your access to site content.

Data Retention and Deletion

Email subscribers can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link included in every email we send. If you would like your email address permanently deleted from our records, contact us at the address below and we will remove it promptly.

Google Analytics data is retained according to Google's default retention settings. This data is aggregated and does not contain personally identifiable information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe from our email list at any time
  • Request deletion of your email address from our systems
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking
  • Access information about what data we hold about you

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and revise the effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
